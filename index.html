Fluffy Homemade Pancakes

Thereâ€™s nothing better than starting your morning with a stack of warm, fluffy pancakes. This recipe is quick, easy, and perfect for lazy weekends.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add wet ingredients.
  3. Cook on a warm pan until golden.
